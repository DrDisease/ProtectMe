Our Project



Context and Objectives

In the current days, the various forms of social media that exist have become a very impactful part of the common person's life, and day by day, we can see a rapidly rising userbase for every social media platform.

And with this growth in usage, there is also a growth in users who try to take advantage of these social media platforms to spread malicious content and deceitful information. Our project aims to monitor the rate at which this kind of content is spread.

In short, we pretend to create an application which, for a given social media post, will try to extract as much information from it as possible and whith this information, categorize the post as either malicious or harmless.
